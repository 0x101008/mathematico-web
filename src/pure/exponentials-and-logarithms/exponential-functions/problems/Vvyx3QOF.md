Find the coordinates of the points of intersection of the curves
$$
y = 4^x
$$
and
$$
y = 9 \times 2^{x} - 8
$$

![Intersecting curves](/img/learn/exp-4.svg)

---

$$
4^x = 2^{2x}
$$

---

$$
2^{2x} = \left( 2^x \right)^2
$$

---

You're dealing with a quadratic equation (in disguise). It factorises.

The function $f$ is defined by
$$
f(x) = 3^x
$$
Find the set of non-negative integers $n \in \mathbb{N}_{0}$ such that the curves $y = f(x)$ and
$$
y = n - nf(-x)
$$
do not intersect.

---

$$
3^x = n - \frac{n}{3^x}
$$
is a quadratic in disguise!

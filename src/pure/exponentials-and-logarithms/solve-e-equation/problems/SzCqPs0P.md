 1. Given that
    $$
    2^{10} = 1024 \approx 1000
    $$
    show that
    $$
    10\ln(2) \approx 3\ln(10)
    $$

 1. Given that
    $$
    e^3 \approx 20
    $$
    show that
    $$
    \ln(2) + \ln(10) \approx 3
    $$

 1. Hence, show that
    $$
    \ln(2) \approx \frac{9}{13}
    $$
    and
    $$
    \ln(10) \approx \frac{30}{13}
    $$

---

For (c), use "simultaneous equations" on the results from (a) and (b).

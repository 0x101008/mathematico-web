The vertex of the parabola
$$
y = x^2 + 6x + c
$$
lies on the parabola
$$
y = x^2
$$
Find the value of $c$.

---

In complete square form, we have
$$
y = (x + 3)^2 - 9 + c
$$

---

So the vertex is at the point
$$
(-3, c-9)
$$
We are told that these coordinates satisfy $y = x^2$

You are given that
$$
x + y = 6
$$
Find the minimum possible value of
$$
2x^2 + y^2
$$

---

$$
x + y = 6 \Rightarrow y = 6 - x
$$

---

Substitute $y = 6 - x$ into $2x^2 + y^2,$ then complete the square to find the minimum value.

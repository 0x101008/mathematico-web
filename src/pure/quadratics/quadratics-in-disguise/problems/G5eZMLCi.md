Solve the equation
$$
2^{2x+1} - 9 \times 2^{x} + 4 = 0
$$

---

This can be written
$$
2 \times \left( 2^x \right)^2 - 9 \times 2^{x} + 4 = 0
$$

---

Let $u = 2^x$ and solve
$$
2u^2 - 9u + 4 = 0
$$

---

### Problem 3

The solutions of

$$
(x+5)^3 - (x-1)^3 = 182
$$

are $\alpha$ and $\beta$.

Find the value of

$$
81 \alpha \beta
$$

<AnswerInput :answer="$frontmatter.answer" />

::: details Hint

Expand and simplify!

:::

::: details Solution

We begin by expanding and simplifying

$$
\begin{aligned}
(x+5)^3 - (x-1)^3 & = 182 \\
(x^3 + 15x^2 + 75x + 125) - (x^3 - 3x^2 + 3x - 1) & = 182 \\
18x^2 + 72x - 56 & = 0 \\
9x^2 + 36x - 28 & = 0 \\
(3x + 14)(3x - 2) & = 0 \\
x & \in \left\{ -\frac{14}{3}, \frac{2}{3} \right\}

\end{aligned}
$$

:::

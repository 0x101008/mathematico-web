The points $P, Q, R$ lie in the plane such that
$$
\begin{aligned}
P & = (6,5) \\[5pt]
Q & = (9, k) \\[5pt]
R & = (11, 2k)
\end{aligned}
$$

The segments $PQ$ and $QR$ form two edges of a rectangle.

Find the possible values of $k.$

---

$PQ$ is perpendicular to $QR$

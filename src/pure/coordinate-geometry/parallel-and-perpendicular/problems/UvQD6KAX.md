$\triangle ABC$ is a triangle in the plane, and angle $\angle ABC$ is a right-angle. Given that
$$
\begin{aligned}
A & = (-3, 1) \\[5pt]
B & = (p, 2p) \\[5pt]
C & = (8, 0)
\end{aligned}
$$
Find the value of $p.$

---

$AB$ is perpendicular to $BC,$ so we know that
$$
m_{AB} \times m_{BC} = -1
$$

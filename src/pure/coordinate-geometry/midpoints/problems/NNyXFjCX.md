The midpoint of
$$
(2,p) \quad \text{and} \quad (p+3,-3)
$$
lies on the line
$$
y = 1 - 2x
$$
Find the value of $p$

---

Find the midpoint in terms of $p$ by applying the formula to $(2,p)$ and $(p+3,-3)$.

The coordinates of the midpoint must satisfy $y = 1 - 2x$.

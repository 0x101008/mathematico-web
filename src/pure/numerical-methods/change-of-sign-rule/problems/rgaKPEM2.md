Let $y = f(x)$ be a continuous curve satisfying
$$
\begin{aligned}
f(0) & = 1 \\
f(1) & = 0
\end{aligned}
$$
Prove that there is some $\alpha,$ where $0 < \alpha < 1,$ such that
$$
 \alpha = f(\alpha)
$$

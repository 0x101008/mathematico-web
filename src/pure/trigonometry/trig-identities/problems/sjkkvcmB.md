Prove the identities

 1. $$
    \tan^2 x + 1 = \frac{1}{\cos^2 x}
    $$

 1. $$
    \frac{\tan^2 \theta - 1}{\sin \theta + \cos \theta} = \frac{\tan \theta - 1}{\cos \theta}
    $$

 1. $$
    \frac{\cos \varphi + \sin \varphi}{1 - \tan^2 \varphi} = \frac{\cos^2 \varphi}{\cos\varphi - \sin\varphi}
    $$

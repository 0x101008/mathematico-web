 1. By considering
    $$
    \tan(A + B) = \frac{\tan A + \tan B}{1 - \tan A \tan B}
    $$
    when $A = x$ and $B = x,$ prove that
    $$
    \tan(2x) = \frac{2 \tan x}{1 - \tan^2 x}
    $$

 1. Hence, or otherwise, solve the equation
    $$
    \tan x \tan 2x = 1, \quad 0 < x < \pi
    $$

The displacement, in meters, traveled by a car at time $t$ seconds is modeled by the function
$$
f(t) = t^2 + t
$$
Find the exact velocity of the car when $t = 3.$

---

Find
$$
\lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \, \frac{f(t+h) - f(t)}{h}
$$

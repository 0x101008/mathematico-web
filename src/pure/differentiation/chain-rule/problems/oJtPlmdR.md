Differentiate the functions

 1. $$
    \sin(\ln(2x+1))
    $$

 1. $$
    e^{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}
    $$

 1. $$
    \ln(\ln(\ln(x)))
    $$

 1. $$
    \cos^2\left( \sqrt{x} \right)
    $$

---

You will need to use the chain rule twice. For (a), the answer is
$$
\cos(\ln(2x+1)) \times \frac{1}{2x + 1} \times 2
$$

Use first principles and small angle approximations to prove that
$$
\begin{aligned}
y & = \sec(x) \\[1em]
\Rightarrow \,\frac{dy}{dx} & = \sec(x) \tan(x)
\end{aligned}
$$

---

Recall that
$$
\sec(x) = \frac{1}{\cos(x)}
$$
You will need the addition formula for $\cos$

Use first principles and small angle approximations to prove that
$$
\begin{aligned}
y & = \tan(x) \\[1em]
\Rightarrow \,\frac{dy}{dx} & = \sec^2(x)
\end{aligned}
$$

---

You will need the addition formula for $\tan$:
$$
\tan(A + B) = \frac{\tan A + \tan B}{1 - \tan A \tan B}
$$

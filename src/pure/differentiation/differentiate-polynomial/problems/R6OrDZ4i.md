$$
f(x) = e^x
$$

$$
f(x) = x^n \, \Rightarrow \, f'(x) = nx^{n-1}
$$

$$
f(x) = e^x \, \Rightarrow \, f'(x) = xe^{x-1}
$$

</blockquote>

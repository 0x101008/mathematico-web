Find the exact value of the gradient of the curve
$$
3^x + y^3 = 3xy
$$
at the point $(2,-3)$.

---

Recall that
$$
\frac{d}{dx} \, a^x = a^x \ln(a)
$$

---

The answer can be given in the form
$$
\frac{3 \ln 3 + 3}{k}, \quad k \in \mathbb{Z}
$$
Give the value of $k$.

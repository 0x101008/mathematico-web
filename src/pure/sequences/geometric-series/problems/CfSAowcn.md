The third and sixth terms of a geometric series are $\frac{9}{16}$ and $\frac{243}{128}$ respectively.

Find the sum of the first $8$ terms of the sequence.

---

$ar^2 = \frac{9}{16}.$

Similar for the sixth term.

---

The sequence $u_n$ is defined by
$$
\begin{aligned}
u_1 & = 3 \\
u_{n+1} & = u_n + 2
\end{aligned}
$$
Find the value of
$$
\sum_{k=1}^{3} S_k
$$
where $S_n$ denotes the $n^{\text{th}}$ partial sum of $u_n.$

---

First calculate $u_1, u_2, u_3,$ and then use them to get $S_1, S_2$ and $S_3.$

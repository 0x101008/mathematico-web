For a given sequence $u_n$ it is known that $S_9 = 75$ and $S_{20} = 325.$

Find the value of
$$
\sum_{k=10}^{20} u_k
$$

---

If
$$
S_4 = u_1 + u_2 + u_3 + u_4
$$
and
$$
S_2 = u_1 + u_2
$$
then
$$
S_4 - S_2 = u_3 + u_4
$$
This same idea can help you solve this problem.

 1. Find
    $$
    \sum_{k=2}^{7} k^2
    $$

 1. Find
    $$
    \sum_{k=2}^{7} k^3
    $$

 1. *Hence*, find
    $$
    \sum_{k=2}^{7} k^2 + k^3
    $$

---

$$
\begin{aligned}
& (2^2 + 2^3) + (3^2 + 3^3) + \ldots + (7^2 + 7^3) \\
= \,\, & (2^2 + 3^2 + \ldots + 7^2) + (2^3 + 3^3 + \ldots + 7^3)
\end{aligned}
$$

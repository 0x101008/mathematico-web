The curve given by
$$
y = (x - a)(x - b)^2
$$
is drawn below.

![Cubic passing through (-2,0) and touching at (3,0)](/img/learn/poly-1.svg)

Find the value of $k.$

---

$x = 3$ is the repeated root.

---

After using the roots to find $a$ and $b,$ let $x = 0$ to find $k.$ (Also, $a$ is not $2.$)

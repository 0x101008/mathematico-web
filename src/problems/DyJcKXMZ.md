Let $a_{1}, a_{2}, \ldots , a_{n}$ be real non-negative real numbers such that $a_{1} \ldots a_{n} = 1$. Show that
$$
(1 + a_{1})(1 + a_{2}) \ldots (1 + a_{n}) \geq 2^{n}
$$

---

Apply the AM-GM inequality to
$$
1, \quad a_1
$$
and also to
$$
1, \quad a_2
$$
and so on...
